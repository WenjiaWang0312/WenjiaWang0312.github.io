<div class="home-intro">
  <p>I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Hong Kong, advised by <a href="https://hku-cg.github.io/">Prof. Taku Komura</a>. My research interests include <strong>Real2Sim</strong>, <strong>Sim2Real</strong>, and <strong>AI for Computer Graphics</strong>.</p>
  <p>Our group is interested in human and spatial intelligence, humanoid simulation, and multimodal human motion understanding and generation. We welcome students who would like to work on theses and research projects targeting top-tier conference publications. If you are interested in collaboration or joining as a student researcher, feel free to contact me via email.</p>
</div>
